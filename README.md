## The Opportunity Cost of Disorganisation
The most valuable thing you have is not your tools or your contacts. It is your attention. When it is spent chasing paperwork, re-entering information you already have, or trying to remember where a job was left, it is not spent on the work that actually moves things forward.

Guidant 365 is built around a simple idea: the people best positioned to grow their work are the ones who are not buried in it. Operational control should not require a dedicated team to maintain it. It should be the starting point.

## What Guidant 365 Does
A Single View of Everything in Motion

Every piece of work has a place, a status, and a clear next step. From the moment a job is enquired about to the moment payment is received, nothing sits in ambiguity. Stages are fully customisable to reflect how your work actually runs, not how a generic template assumes it does.

## Documents That Work For You

Upload a photo, a PDF, or a scanned document. Guidant 365 reads it, identifies what matters, and surfaces those details for your review. You decide what gets saved. The information that was previously locked inside a file becomes part of your operational picture, visible and actionable.

<img src="assets/screenshots/approval-queue.png" alt="Approval queue" width="100%">

## Communication That Stays Consistent

Connect your Gmail account with the level of access you are comfortable with. Guidant 365 reads incoming correspondence in context and prepares suggested replies in your choice of tone. You review, refine if needed, and approve. Client communication stays considered and on time, even when you are busy.

## Cashflow You Can See Coming

A full calendar view brings jobs, payment due dates, upcoming commitments, and recurring obligations into one coherent picture. Overdue items surface before they become problems. Knowing where things stand financially is no longer something you do at the end of the month. It is something you have every day.

<img src="assets/screenshots/calendar.png" alt="Calendar month view" width="100%">

## Client Relationships With Memory

Every client account holds a complete history: correspondence, files, quotes, notes, and status changes across every engagement. The context that usually lives in someone's inbox or memory lives here instead, organised, searchable, and permanent.

## The Value of Being Ahead
There is a version of running your work where you are always slightly behind. Quotes not yet sent, invoices not yet followed up, details not yet recorded. Most people accept that version because the alternative seemed to require more time than they had.

Guidant 365 is the alternative. Not because it does everything, but because it handles the right things quietly, keeps a record of all of it, and puts your attention back where it belongs.

The subscription pays for itself the first time a payment is collected on time that would otherwise have been forgotten, or the first time a client receives a reply that lands well because you had the context in front of you.
